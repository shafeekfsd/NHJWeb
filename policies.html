Quality Policy

NHJ Contracting Company is committed to delivering comprehensive Electro-Mechanical and Construction solutions of the highest quality.

In pursuit of this commitment, we have implemented a Quality Management System that aligns with both International and Saudi Standards, ensuring full compliance with quality assurance requirements in production, installation, and servicing in accordance with our suppliers’ standards and accreditation.

We firmly believe that economic success and customer satisfaction are achieved through maintaining the highest levels of performance, reliability, and quality. Every employee within NHJ Contracting Company is dedicated to implementing and upholding the company’s quality requirements within the scope of their responsibilities. NHJ Contracting Company is fully committed to compliance with ISO 9001 and other relevant international quality standards.

OH & S Policy

The Management of NHJ Contracting Company firmly believes that every employee has the right to work in a safe and healthy workplace.

To achieve this, the Company will make every reasonable effort in the areas of accident prevention, hazard control and elimination, injury protection, and health preservation and promotion. These key aspects of workplace safety will always receive top priority in our company plans, procedures, programs, and job instructions.

In alignment with this policy, NHJ Contracting Company will develop and implement a series of rules, procedures, programs, and policy statements addressing specific safety and health matters. The Company is fully committed to complying with the requirements of ISO 45001 and other relevant Occupational Health and Safety (OH&S) standards.

Environmental Policy

We recognize our responsibility to the environment and are committed to minimizing our ecological footprint. We execute projects with a sustainable approach, ensuring strict adherence to relevant regulations and standards. We strive to reduce waste, conserve resources, and promote environmental awareness among our employees and partners.
