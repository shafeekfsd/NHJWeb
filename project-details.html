Project Overview

The Al Khobar Industrial Complex represents a landmark achievement in modern industrial infrastructure development. This comprehensive project encompasses state-of-the-art facilities designed to support the growing industrial sector in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

Our team successfully delivered a fully integrated industrial complex featuring advanced manufacturing facilities, warehousing solutions, and supporting infrastructure. The project was completed on schedule and within budget, demonstrating our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.

Key features include energy-efficient design, sustainable construction practices, and cutting-edge safety systems. The complex is equipped with modern fire protection systems, advanced HVAC solutions, and comprehensive electrical infrastructure to support diverse industrial operations.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

Complex site conditions with varying soil composition requiring specialized foundation solutions.

Solution

Implemented advanced geotechnical engineering and customized foundation design for optimal stability.
