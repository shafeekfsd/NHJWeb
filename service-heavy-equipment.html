Service Overview

NHJ Contracting Company provides a comprehensive fleet of heavy rental equipment designed to meet the demanding needs of construction, industrial, and infrastructure projects across Saudi Arabia.

Our extensive inventory includes excavators, bulldozers, cranes, loaders, and specialized machinery, all maintained to the highest standards. We ensure that every piece of equipment is regularly serviced, inspected, and ready for immediate deployment.

With flexible rental terms, competitive pricing, and 24/7 technical support, we help our clients complete their projects on time and within budget.
